About our Project

MultiLine Tetris is an adaption to one of the most known games of the world: Tetris.

We tried to create a competitive game out of it for up to 4 players.

The project was started for Ubiqutious Media lecture, held by Prof. Antonio Krüger

A project by Sebastian Schirmer, Kevin Presser and Nikodemus Schmidt

Saarland University, 2013
